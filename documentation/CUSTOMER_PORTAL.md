# Customer Portal

Routes:

- `/portal`
- `/portal/products`
- `/portal/licenses`
- `/portal/orders`
- `/portal/downloads`
- `/portal/support`
- `/portal/notifications`
- `/portal/settings`
- `/portal/settings/discord`

The portal displays:

- Active licenses.
- Products owned.
- Recent orders.
- Open tickets.
- Product updates.
- Announcements.
- Discord account connection status.
